## Overview

A full-stack custom ERP platform developed for T-Works that streamlines business processes and operational workflows. The platform handles both internal requirements and client-side functionalities, enabling seamless business process management with comprehensive ticket tracking and follow-up capabilities.

## Key Features

- **Dual-sided Requirements**: Manages both internal operations and client-facing workflows with role-based access control.
- **Ticket Tracking System**: Comprehensive ticket management with automated tracking and escalation workflows.
- **Follow-up Management**: Built-in follow-up system for service management and client issue resolution.
- **Business Process Automation**: Streamlined workflows for operational efficiency and reduced manual tasks.
- **Client Management**: Integrated client portal for issue reporting and status tracking.
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Real-time dashboards and comprehensive reporting for business insights.

## Technologies Used

- **Full Stack Development**: Modern web technologies for robust and scalable architecture
- **Database Management**: Efficient data handling for enterprise-level operations
- **API Integration**: RESTful APIs for seamless internal and external integrations
- **Authentication & Authorization**: Secure access control with role-based permissions

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was designing a system that could handle both complex internal workflows and user-friendly client interactions simultaneously. Implementing a scalable ticket tracking system that could grow with the company's needs while maintaining performance required careful architecture planning and database optimization.

## Outcome

The ERP platform successfully unified T-Works' operations, reducing manual administrative tasks and improving operational efficiency. The integrated ticket tracking system enhanced service delivery and client satisfaction by providing transparent issue resolution tracking.
